MimicDB

MimicDB is the repository for information about the 24 human dimeric integrins. It contains all sequence-level, structural, and expression information currently known about integrins. MimicDB also holds information about known disintegrins, which are snake venom proteins, known to target certain types of integrins to inhibit cellular interactions (most notably blood clotting) of the prey. The database also lists all currently available drugs in clinical use that target integrins.

MimicDB is the platform for the data collected in the course of the EMBO|EuropaBio fellowship 7544 for Balint Meszaros. It will also serve as a database for the dissemination of the data collected and results generated in the course of potential future research with MSCA/EMBL funding.

The current version of MimicDB is for monitoring the progress of the ongoing project, and for evaluation purposes of grant proposals for potential future funding. It is not intended for public use in its current form.

MimicDB is developed and maintained by Marc Gouw (Gibson Team, EMBL Heidelberg)
